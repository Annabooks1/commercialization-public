## Running the test


Before you run the test, complete the test setup as described in the test requirements: [Mouse or other Pointing Device Testing Prerequisites](mouse-or-other-pointing-device-testing-prerequisites.md).

1.  If the touchpad surface is at all above the surrounding casing of the touchpad, press **F** fail the test. Else, move to the next step.

2.  Place a 1.5mm wide piece of plastic (or other non-conductive material) on the surface of the touchpad, such that the plastic does not touch the surrounding casing of the touchpad.

    -   If the touchpad and the plastic is lower than the surrounding casing, press **F** to fail the test. If the touchpad surface is anywhere, press **P** to pass.
