# Test.Edge


This test verifies that device properly registers a swipe in from the left and right edges, starting outside the surface of the device and moving inward.

## Running the test


Before you run the test, complete the test setup as described in the test requirements: [Mouse or other Pointing Device Testing Prerequisites](mouse-or-other-pointing-device-testing-prerequisites.md).

1.  Start the test. Wait at least 10 seconds (there is a timer in the top right corner of the screen for reference).

2.  Swipe in horizontally from the edge specified by the instructions on the screen.

    -   Your swipe should start off the edge of the touchpad, and swipe inwards (keeping your fingertip flush with the device) toward the center of the touchpad at 200 mm/s. When you reach 10-20mm past the edge of the touchpad, lift your contact.

        -   The tool will automatically pass/fail the iteration if the requirement is met.

3.  Repeat step 2 for a total of 20 iterations (10 on left edge and 10 on right).

## Troubleshooting


For troubleshooting information, see [Troubleshooting Device.Input Testing](troubleshooting-deviceinput-testing.md).

If you see error “\[14\] packet too far from edge: \#\#\#”, ensure that the swipe starts at the edge (or before it) and you are swiping from the appropriate edge, as specified by the test.
