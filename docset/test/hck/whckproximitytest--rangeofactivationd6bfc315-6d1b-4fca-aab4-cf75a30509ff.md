# WHCKProximityTest- RangeOfActivation


This manual test determines whether the implementation recognizes NFC enabled devices when they com into range of detection.

To successfully complete this test, you must respoind to prompts that appear.

When prompted, bring a second proximity device into range, even though no WHCK test is running on the second device or its computer.
