# WHCKProximityDeviceTest - RangeOfActivation


This test verifies that the devices interact within a specified range of distances.

During the test, you receive the following message:

**Move the devices within 4 cm of each other NOW**

To complete the test, move both devices within 4 centimeters of each other.
